## About This Edition
This annotated edition of Niccolò Machiavelli’s *The Prince* is based on the public‑domain English translation by Charles Hildreth (1820). The source text has been verified as public domain via Project Gutenberg (ebook #1232). Annotations aim to clarify historical references, archaic terms, and political concepts for modern readers while preserving the original wording.
